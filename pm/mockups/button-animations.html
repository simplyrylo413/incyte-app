Button click animations

Inline · INCYTE tokens · ≤360ms
1 · Press-scale (current default)
100ms cubic-bezier scale(0.95) on press. Already used everywhere — back chip, hero numerics, BW/WU/WS toggles, bottom-nav, equipment pill.
2 · Translate-down press
Button drops 2px on press, shadow softens. Reads as a physical key press. Good for primary action buttons that already have a real drop shadow.
3 · Accent ripple pulse
Single steel-blue ring expands 10px and fades over 320ms. One-shot, finite. Best fit for primary CTAs — Log set, Apply, Finish.
4 · Border flash to accent
Border + text briefly shift to --accent on press. Best for toggle / chip buttons where state is the message.
5 · Inset press shadow
Inset shadow + slight scale + faint accent tint. Reads as "pushed in." Good for hero numerics (weight × reps × rpe).
× ×
6 · Tap-origin ripple (Material-style)
Ripple expands from the exact tap point. Requires ~10 lines of vanilla JS to position the ring. Use sparingly — bigger surfaces only.
7 · Success-tick punch
Checkmark scales up to 1.22 and settles back. Trigger by adding .just-logged class for one render, then removing. Pairs with set logging.
Tap to fire the punch
8 · Damped cubic spring (no library)
Multi-keyframe scale 1 → 0.9 → 1.06 → 0.98 → 1 over 360ms with a slight overshoot easing. The closest in-CSS approximation to motion.dev's spring() without a CDN dep.
Tap each button to feel the effect. Spec: ≤360ms, --ease-out-cubic, finite, all INCYTE tokens.
