Rest timer pill — three options

Each option shown in three states: OFF (toggle off, time = current preset), ON (running, live countdown), and URGENT (last 10 seconds — mauve alert). Pick a direction.

Inline switch

Pill contains REST label + time + a tiny iOS switch at the right end. The switch IS the toggle. Most "settings-like" affordance.
